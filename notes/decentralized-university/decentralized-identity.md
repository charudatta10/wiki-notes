# [Decentralized Identity](Decentralized Identity.md)

[Decentralized Identity](Decentralized Identity.md) (DID) is a trust framework in which identifiers are self-sovereign, independent, and enable trusted data exchange.

In the context of a [decentralized university](decentralized university.md), DIDs could be used for:

*   **Students and Faculty:** To control their own academic records and credentials.
*   **Reputation:** To build a verifiable reputation based on contributions to the system.
*   **Authentication:** To securely access university resources and services without a central authority.
