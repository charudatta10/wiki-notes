# [P2P Journal Idea](P2P Journal Idea.md)

This document outlines an idea for a [p2p-journal](p2p-journal.md) system.

## Core Concepts

*   **Separation of Methodology and Results:** Papers are split into two distinct parts: the methodology and the results. This allows for independent verification.
*   **Independent, Anonymous, Open, Time-Bound Review:**
    *   **Independent:** Reviewers are not chosen by the author.
    *   **Anonymous:** Reviews are double-blind.
    *   **Open:** Reviews are publicly visible after a certain period.
    *   **Time-Bound:** The review process has a strict deadline.
*   **Federated Machine Learning:** Utilize federated machine learning for tasks like plagiarism detection and finding suitable reviewers without centralizing data.
