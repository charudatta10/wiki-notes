# [AI in Note Taking](AI in Note Taking.md)

[Artificial Intelligence](Artificial Intelligence.md) ([AI](AI.md)) is increasingly being integrated into [note-taking](note-taking.md) tools to enhance [productivity](productivity.md) and knowledge management. [AI](AI.md) features can include automatic transcription, summarization of notes, intelligent linking of related concepts, natural language processing for search, and even generating insights from collected information.
