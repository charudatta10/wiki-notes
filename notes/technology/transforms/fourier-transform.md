# [Fourier Transform](Fourier Transform.md)

The [Fourier Transform](Fourier Transform.md) is a mathematical transform that decomposes a function (often a function of time, or a [signal-processing](signal-processing.md)) into its constituent frequencies. It's widely used in [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), image processing, and many other fields to analyze the frequency content of [signal-processing](signal-processing.md).
