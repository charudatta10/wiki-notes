# [Z-Transform](Z-Transform.md)

The [Z-Transform](Z-Transform.md) is a mathematical tool that converts a discrete-time signal into a complex frequency-domain representation. It is the discrete-time equivalent of the [Laplace Transform](Laplace Transform.md) and is widely used in digital [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), control theory, and difference equations to analyze and design discrete-time systems.
