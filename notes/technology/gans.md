# [GANs](GANs.md)

Generative Adversarial Networks ([GANs](GANs.md)) are a class of artificial intelligence algorithms used in unsupervised machine learning. They consist of two neural networks, a generator and a discriminator, competing against each other. [GANs](GANs.md) are primarily used to generate new data instances that resemble the training data, such as images, music, or text.
