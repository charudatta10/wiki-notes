# [[RF Parameters]]

[[Radio Frequency]] ([[RF]]) systems are characterized by several key [[RF Parameters]] that define their performance and behavior. These include:

*   **Frequency:** The rate of oscillation of the electromagnetic wave, typically measured in Hertz (Hz).
*   **Wavelength:** The spatial period of the wave, inversely proportional to frequency.
*   **Power:** The strength of the [[RF]] [[signal-processing]], often measured in Watts (W) or dBm.
*   **Bandwidth:** The range of frequencies occupied by a [[signal-processing]] or that a system can process.
*   **Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR):** A measure comparing the level of a desired [[signal-processing]] to the level of background noise.
