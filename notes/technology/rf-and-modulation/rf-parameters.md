# [RF Parameters](RF Parameters.md)

[Radio Frequency](Radio Frequency.md) ([RF](RF.md)) systems are characterized by several key [RF Parameters](RF Parameters.md) that define their performance and behavior. These include:

*   **Frequency:** The rate of oscillation of the electromagnetic wave, typically measured in Hertz (Hz).
*   **Wavelength:** The spatial period of the wave, inversely proportional to frequency.
*   **Power:** The strength of the [RF](RF.md) [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), often measured in Watts (W) or dBm.
*   **Bandwidth:** The range of frequencies occupied by a [signal-processing](signal-processing.md) or that a system can process.
*   **Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR):** A measure comparing the level of a desired [signal-processing](signal-processing.md) to the level of background noise.
