# [Demodulation](Demodulation.md)

[Demodulation](Demodulation.md) is the process of extracting the original information-bearing [signal-processing](signal-processing.md) from a modulated carrier wave. It is the reverse process of [modulation](modulation.md). Demodulators are circuits or algorithms designed to recover the baseband [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), making the information accessible for its intended use (e.g., audio from a radio [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), data from a wireless transmission).
