# [Quadrature Amplitude Modulation](Quadrature Amplitude Modulation.md) ([QAM](QAM.md))

[Quadrature Amplitude Modulation](Quadrature Amplitude Modulation.md) ([QAM](QAM.md)) is a [modulation](modulation.md) scheme that conveys data by changing (modulating) the amplitude of two carrier waves, 90 degrees out of phase with each other. These two modulated waves are combined and transmitted. [QAM](QAM.md) is commonly used in digital wireless communication systems and for high-speed data transmission over limited bandwidth.
