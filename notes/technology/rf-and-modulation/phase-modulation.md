# [Phase Modulation](Phase Modulation.md) (PM)

[Phase Modulation](Phase Modulation.md) (PM) is a [modulation](modulation.md) mode used for transmitting information by varying the instantaneous phase of a carrier wave. The amplitude and frequency of the carrier remain constant. PM is closely related to [Frequency Modulation](Frequency Modulation.md) (FM) and is often used in digital communication systems and for generating FM signals.
