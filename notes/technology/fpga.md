# [FPGA](FPGA.md)

A [Field-Programmable Gate Array](Field-Programmable Gate Array.md) ([FPGA](FPGA.md)) is an integrated circuit designed to be configured by a customer or a designer after manufacturing. [FPGAs](FPGAs.md) are reconfigurable and can implement any digital function. They are commonly used for prototyping ASICs, in digital [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), and in high-performance computing.
