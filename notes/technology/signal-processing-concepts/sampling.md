# [Sampling](Sampling.md)

[Sampling](Sampling.md) is the process of converting a continuous-time analog [signal-processing](signal-processing.md) into a discrete-time digital [signal-processing](signal-processing.md). This is done by taking measurements of the [signal-processing](signal-processing.md)'s amplitude at regular intervals. The [sampling](sampling.md) rate (or [sampling](sampling.md) frequency) is crucial, as dictated by the Nyquist-Shannon [sampling](sampling.md) theorem, to accurately reconstruct the original [signal-processing](signal-processing.md).
