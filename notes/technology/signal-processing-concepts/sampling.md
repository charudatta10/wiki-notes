# [[Sampling]]

[[Sampling]] is the process of converting a continuous-time analog [[signal-processing]] into a discrete-time digital [[signal-processing]]. This is done by taking measurements of the [[signal-processing]]'s amplitude at regular intervals. The [[sampling]] rate (or [[sampling]] frequency) is crucial, as dictated by the Nyquist-Shannon [[sampling]] theorem, to accurately reconstruct the original [[signal-processing]].
