# [Convolution](Convolution.md)

[Convolution](Convolution.md) is a mathematical operation on two functions (f and g) that produces a third function, expressing how the shape of one is modified by the other. In [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), [convolution](convolution.md) is used to describe the relationship between an input [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), a system's impulse response, and its output [signal-processing](signal-processing.md). It's fundamental to understanding linear time-invariant (LTI) systems.
