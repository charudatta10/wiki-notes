# [Modulation](Modulation.md)

[Modulation](Modulation.md) is the process of varying one or more properties of a periodic waveform, called the carrier [signal-processing](signal-processing.md), with a modulating [signal-processing](signal-processing.md) that typically contains information to be transmitted. It's a key technique in telecommunications to transmit information over long distances, such as amplitude [modulation](modulation.md) (AM) and frequency [modulation](modulation.md) (FM).
