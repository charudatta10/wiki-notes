# [[Note Taking]]

Effective [[note-taking]] is crucial for learning and retaining information. Here are a few popular methods:

*   **The [[Cornell Method]]:** A system for recording, organizing, and reviewing notes.
*   **The [[Zettelkasten]] Method:** A method for networked thought, where notes are linked together.
*   **Mind Mapping:** A visual way to organize information.
