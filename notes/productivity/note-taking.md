# [Note Taking](Note Taking.md)

Effective [note-taking](note-taking.md) is crucial for learning and retaining information. Here are a few popular methods:

*   **The [Cornell Method](Cornell Method.md):** A system for recording, organizing, and reviewing notes.
*   **The [Zettelkasten](Zettelkasten.md) Method:** A method for networked thought, where notes are linked together.
*   **Mind Mapping:** A visual way to organize information.
