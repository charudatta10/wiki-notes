# [Productivity](Productivity.md)

[Productivity](Productivity.md) is about efficiently achieving goals. It's not just about doing more, but about doing the right things.

Some [productivity](productivity.md) techniques include:

*   **The Pomodoro Technique:** A time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals.
*   **Time Blocking:** A method of scheduling out every part of your day.
*   **The 2-Minute Rule:** If a task takes less than two minutes, do it now.
