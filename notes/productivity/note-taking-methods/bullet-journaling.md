# [Bullet Journaling](Bullet Journaling.md)

[Bullet Journaling](Bullet Journaling.md) is a customizable organizational system developed by Ryder Carroll. It combines elements of a diary, [task-management](task-management.md), and sketchbook into a single notebook. It uses "bullets" to quickly log tasks, events, and [note-taking](note-taking.md), and encourages reflection and intentional living.
