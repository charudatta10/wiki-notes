# [Zettelkasten](Zettelkasten.md)

The [Zettelkasten](Zettelkasten.md) method is a personal knowledge management and [note-taking](note-taking.md) system used for research and writing. It involves creating individual [note-taking](note-taking.md) (zettel) with unique IDs, linking them to related [note-taking](note-taking.md), and developing a network of interconnected ideas. This method encourages deep thinking and the generation of new insights.
