# [Epistemology](Epistemology.md)

[Epistemology](Epistemology.md) is the branch of philosophy concerned with knowledge. It addresses questions such as:

*   What is knowledge?
*   How is knowledge acquired?
*   What do people know?
*   What are the limits of what we can know?

Key concepts in [epistemology](epistemology.md) include justification, truth, and belief.
